## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Usage Time Limits:** Time limit settings you configure for YouTube usage
- **PIN/Password:** Local authentication credentials for parental controls (stored locally on device only)

### Information Collected Automatically

- **App Usage Data:** Time spent on YouTube application (collected locally to enforce time limits)
- **Device Information:** Device type and Android version (for compatibility purposes only)

### Permissions Required

The App requires the following permissions to function:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| INTERNET | Required for basic app functionality |
| ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE | Check network connectivity status |
| SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW | Display overlay notifications when time limits are reached |
| PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS | Monitor YouTube app usage time to enforce limits |
| RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED | Start monitoring service after device restart |
| FOREGROUND_SERVICE | Run background service for continuous monitoring |
| BIND_DEVICE_ADMIN | Enable device administrator features for enhanced parental controls |
| GET_TASKS | Detect currently running applications |
| WAKE_LOCK | Keep monitoring service active |

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

1. **Enforcing Time Limits:** Monitoring YouTube usage and enforcing the time limits you set
2. **Parental Controls:** Preventing unauthorized changes to settings via PIN protection
3. **Service Functionality:** Ensuring the App works correctly after device restarts
4. **App Improvement:** Understanding general usage patterns to improve the App (no personal data shared)

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only

**All data is stored locally on your device.** We do not:
- Upload your data to external servers
- Share your data with third parties
- Collect personally identifiable information
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, messages, or other personal content

### Security Measures

- PIN/Password data is stored securely on your device
- No cloud synchronization or backup of personal settings
- Data remains on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.**

Your data may only be disclosed if:
- Required by law or legal process
- Necessary to protect our rights or safety
- You provide explicit consent

## Children's Privacy

The YouTube Limiter App is designed as a parental control tool. While the App may be installed on devices used by children:

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
- The App is intended to be configured and managed by parents/guardians
- No data is transmitted from the device to external servers
