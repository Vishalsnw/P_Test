## Full Description (4000 chars max)

```
Take control of your family's screen time with YouTube Limiter - the simple and effective parental control app designed to help children develop healthy digital habits.

MANAGE SCREEN TIME EASILY

Set daily time limits for YouTube, games, and other apps. Once the limit is reached, the app automatically blocks access, helping your child balance screen time with other activities.

KEY FEATURES

Daily Time Limits
Set custom daily usage limits for YouTube and other apps. Choose anywhere from 15 minutes to several hours based on your family's needs.

App Usage Monitoring
Track how much time your child spends on YouTube, games, social media, and other applications. Get a clear picture of their digital habits.

Multiple App Support
Not just YouTube! Set limits for games, social media apps, streaming services, and any other apps you want to control.

PIN Protected Settings
Secure all settings with a PIN code that only parents know. Prevent children from changing time limits or disabling the app.

Auto-Start on Boot
The app automatically starts monitoring when the device restarts, ensuring continuous protection without manual intervention.

Gentle Notifications
Receive friendly reminders when time is running low, giving children a chance to wrap up their activity before the limit is reached.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Install the app and set your PIN
2. Select the apps you want to limit (YouTube, games, etc.)
3. Set daily time limits for each app
4. The app monitors usage and blocks access when limits are reached
5. Limits reset automatically each day

WHY CHOOSE YOUTUBE LIMITER?

Simple Setup
Get started in minutes with an easy-to-use interface designed for busy parents.

No Account Required
Works immediately without creating accounts or subscriptions. Your privacy is protected.

Offline Functionality
Works completely offline. No internet required for core functionality.

Battery Efficient
Optimized to use minimal battery while providing reliable monitoring.

Free to Use
Core features are completely free with no hidden costs.

PERFECT FOR

- Parents wanting to limit YouTube and gaming time
- Families looking to establish healthy screen time habits
- Managing multiple apps on children's devices
- Creating a balanced digital lifestyle for kids

PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED

This app requires certain permissions to function:
- Usage Access: To monitor app usage time
- Display Over Apps: To show time limit notifications
- Device Admin: For enhanced parental control features
- Run at Startup: To start monitoring automatically

Having issues or suggestions? We'd love to hear from you! Contact us through the app or leave a review.
```
